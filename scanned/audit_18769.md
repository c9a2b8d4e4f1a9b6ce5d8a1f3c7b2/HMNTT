# Risk of overflow.

## Summary
The calculation used in the accTokenPerShare feature can cause an overflow error due to the use of large numbers. This can happen when the pool rate is high and the rateDenominator is low. To fix this, the overflow error should be handled or prevented.

## Details
**Description**


accTokenPerShare calculation utilizes 1e12*1e18*1e32 that is equal to 1e62. Max size of
unsigned integer (256) is 2**256-1 that is ~1e77. With high pool rate and low
rateDenominator such amount can cause an overflow.

**Recommendation**:

Handle or prevent an overflow exception.
