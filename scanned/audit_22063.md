# Use SafeMath in all math operations

## Summary
This bug report is about an issue with math operations in a software system. It states that there are a few operations that are unchecked, and provides an example of two of them. It is always better to use checked operations to avoid any potential issues. 

The bug report was updated with a response from Hubii, who said that they had updated the software to use safe math operations where they were not used previously. This ensures that all operations are checked and safe to use.

## Details
Most math operations are safe, but there’s still a few that are unchecked ([these two](https://github.com/CoinFabrik/ico/blob/142f43f3bec4afd0ffff3fce06946cf67cb0272f/contracts/Crowdsale.sol#L241), for instance). It’s always better to be safe and perform checked operations.


**Update:** Hubii replied *“We updated to use safe math operations where they were not used.”*
