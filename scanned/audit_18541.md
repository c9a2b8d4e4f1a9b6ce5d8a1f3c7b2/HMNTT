# Revert during price deduction

## Summary
The report describes a bug in the CryptoBearWatchClub contract, specifically in the functions dutchAuctionForPresale() and dutchAuctionForPublicMint(). The bug is caused by the expression "uint256 price = startingPrice - priceDeduction", which limits the length of the auction. As time passes, the priceDeduction value increases and may eventually become higher than the price, causing the contract to fail due to an underflow error. The recommendation is to add conditions to handle this edge case or set a maximum value for priceDeduction to prevent it from exceeding the price.

## Details
**Description**

CryptoBearWatchClub.sol:
dutchAuctionForPresale(), dutchAuctionForPublicMint() Auction length is limited because of
the expression “uint256 price = startingPrice - priceDeduction”
priceDeduction increases with time, thus it will reach the point when it becomes higher than
the price and the contract will revert because of the underflow.

**Recommendation**:

Add conditions to resolve the edge case or set the max for the priceDeduction so it will not
overflow the price.
