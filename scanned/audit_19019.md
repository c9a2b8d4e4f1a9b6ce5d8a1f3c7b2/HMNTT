# Account remains connected to previous credit manager

## Summary
This bug report is about an issue with the Gearbox Protocol's Account Factory. After returning, the credit account is still connected to the previous credit manager instead of the factory. This issue can be resolved by connecting the account to the factory after returning. This will ensure that the account is connected to the factory instead of the previous credit manager.

## Details
##### Description
After returning, credit account is still connected to previous credit manager:
https://github.com/Gearbox-protocol/gearbox-contracts/blob/0ac33ba87212ce056ac6b6357ad74161d417158a/contracts/core/AccountFactory.sol#L196
##### Recommendation
We recommend connecting account to factory after returning.
