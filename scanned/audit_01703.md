# [M-09] Desync lets bribes be received without emissions

## Summary
This bug report discusses an issue with the BribeVotingReward system, which rewards voters based on their voting power. Due to a timing issue, some voters may switch their vote before their weight affects the reward, resulting in them receiving bribes but the bribing protocols not receiving the emissions. The report recommends allowing a 1-hour window after voting for protocols to trigger distribution to solve this issue. The severity and likelihood of this bug are both considered medium.

## Details
## Severity

**Impact:** Medium

**Likelihood:** Medium

## Description

Because of the fact that BribeVotingRewardwill award bribes based on the voting power at EPOCH_-
END - 1, but Minter.update_period() and Voter.distribute() can be called at a time after, some voters may
switch their vote before their weight influences emissions, causing the voters to receive bribes, but the bribing
protocols to not have their gauge receive the emissions.

## Recommendations

 Allowing 1 hour post-voting window for protocols to trigger distribution.
