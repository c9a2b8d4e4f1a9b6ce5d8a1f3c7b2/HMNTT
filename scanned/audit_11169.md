# Low Test Coverage

## Summary
The client was not able to meet their internal deadline for achieving 90% test coverage. They focused on critical tests and ignored view functions, resulting in low overall branch coverage. It is important to have high test coverage to reduce the risk of unexpected behaviors. The recommendation is to reach at least 90% branch coverage before deploying to production.
