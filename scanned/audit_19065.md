# Incorrect calculation of borrowed amount

## Summary
This bug report is about a problem with the calculation of borrowed amount for credit accounts in the Gearbox Protocol. The issue is that the total borrowed amount increases unequally, which means that the total borrowed amount on credit accounts would be less than the totalBorrowed amount on a PoolService. This would lead to incorrect calculations for the Liquidity Pool of a PoolService.

To fix this issue, it is recommended to change the calculation of borrowed amount for credit accounts. This would ensure that the total borrowed amount is accurate and that the Liquidity Pool of a PoolService is correctly calculated.

## Details
##### Description
Total borrowed amount increases unequally, so total borrowed amount on credit accounts would be less than `totalBorrowed` on a `PoolService` which would lead to incorrect calcultaions for LP of a `PoolService`:
https://github.com/Gearbox-protocol/gearbox-contracts/blob/0ac33ba87212ce056ac6b6357ad74161d417158a/contracts/credit/CreditManager.sol#L661
##### Recommendation
We recommend to change calculation of borrowed amount for credit accounts.
