# Facade allows any unauthorized Executor

## Summary
This bug report is about the SwapFacade, a contract that allows users to choose any SwapExecutor address, even if it is not authored by the team. Fees are charged on the SwapExecutor, so it is possible to fork the SwapExecutor with zero fees and choose it as an executor for swaps. This could lead to users choosing out of date executors or executors with bugs, which is not ideal. 

The recommendation is to have a list of allowed executors and to take fees on SwapFacade instead. This would ensure that only up to date and bug-free SwapExecutors are chosen, making the process more secure.
