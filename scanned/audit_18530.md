# SRP. Method `mint` of Smart contract CryptoBearWatchClub has misleading name as it used for running auction sale and not just mint.

## Summary
The report suggests that the functionality of minting tokens should be separated into two different methods - one for the Smart contract owner and another for auction sales. This will help improve the overall efficiency of the process.

## Details
**Recommendation**:
Split mint by Smart contract owner functionality and auction sale to two diffrent methods.
