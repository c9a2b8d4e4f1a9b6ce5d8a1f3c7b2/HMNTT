# Work with incorrect decimals

## Summary
This bug report concerns the PriceOracle.sol contract from the Gearbox Protocol. This contract is responsible for providing price feeds for certain tokens. The issue here is that the contract is returning prices with less than 18 decimals.

The recommendation is to check the decimals of the `priceFeeds[token]` to ensure that the correct number of decimals is being returned. This is important to ensure accuracy in the price feed and avoid any discrepancies.
