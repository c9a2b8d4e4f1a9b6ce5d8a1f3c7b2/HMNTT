# Emergency admin can transfer aWETH from the `PositionManager`

## Summary
The report highlights an issue with the `EMERGENCY_ROLE` in the `PositionManager` contract. This role allows for the transfer of aWETH to any address without restrictions, which could be risky if the role is compromised. The recommendation is to disallow emergency transfers or approvals from the `PositionManager` contract, as withdrawals can already be done through a more transparent process. The client notes that the `EMERGENCY_ROLE` will only be assigned to the highest level of multisig authority and will not be configured at launch.

## Details
##### Description
`EMERGENCY_ROLE` in `PositionManager` can transfer aWETH from the contract to any address without restrictions, which may be unintended and creates additional risks in case this role is compromised.

##### Recommendation
We recommend disallowing aWETH emergency transfers or approvals from the `PositionManager` contract, since withdrawals are already handled via the more transparent `withdraw()` flow.

> **Client's Commentary:**
> This is an emergency function intended for rapid fund withdrawal in critical situations. The `EMERGENCY_ROLE` will not be configured at launch. It will be assigned only to the highest level of multisig authority.
