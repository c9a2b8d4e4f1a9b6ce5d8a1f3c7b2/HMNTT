# Privileged Roles and Ownership

## Summary
The client has acknowledged a bug in the smart contract code and has explained that the `TOKEN_SAVER_ROLE` allows for privileged accounts to send any token from the contract to any account in any amount. This role can be granted by accounts with `DEFAULT_ADMIN_ROLE` control. Additionally, there are other roles such as `GOV_ROLE`, `REWARD_DISTRIBUTOR_ROLE`, `MIGRATION_TRANSFER_ROLE`, and `WHITELIST_TRANSFER_ROLE` that grant similar privileges. The recommendation is to make this centralization of power clear to users and consider removing the `TOKEN_SAVER_ROLE` or implementing a multisig system for handling it.

## Details
**Update**
Marked as "Acknowledged" by the client. Addressed in: `599e4cf98403d99f48b25ce42ce412747cd368f5`. The client provided the following explanation:

> We are aware of the privileged roles and elevated capability of TOKEN_SAVER_ROLE which we might use in cases of another migration/upgrade

**Description:** Smart contracts will often have `owner` variables to designate the person with special privileges to make modifications to the smart contract.

1.   `TokenSaver`
    1.   Accounts granted the `TOKEN_SAVER_ROLE` can call `saveToken()`, which allows them to **send any token from the contract to any account in any amount**. Note that accounts with `DEFAULT_ADMIN_ROLE` control can grant the `TOKEN_SAVER_ROLE`.

2.   `BasePool`
    1.   Accounts granted the `GOV_ROLE` can call `updateEscrowDuration()`, which allows them to change the duration of the escrow period.

3.   `BreederLiquidityMiningManager`
    1.   Accounts granted the `GOV_ROLE` can call
        1.   `addPool()`
        2.   `removePool()`
        3.   `adjustWeight()`
        4.   `setRewardPerSecond()`
        5.   `setVariableSource()`
        6.   `updateFixedRewardSource()`

    2.   Accounts granted the `REWARD_DISTRIBUTOR_ROLE` can call `distributeRewards()`

4.   `BreederMigrationPool`
    1.   Accounts granted the `MIGRATION_TRANSFER_ROLE` can call
        1.   `batchMigrationDeposit()`
        2.   `disableMigration()`

5.   `BreederWhitelistPool`
    1.   Accounts granted the `WHITELIST_TRANSFER_ROLE` can call `breedWhitelistTransfer()`, which allows them to **burn the deposits of any user**.

6.   `BreederTimeLockPool`
    1.   Accounts granted the `GOV_ROLE` can call
        1.   `setStakingBonus()`
        2.   `setBreederlzAdress()`

**Recommendation:** This centralization of power needs to be made clear to the users, especially depending on the level of privilege the contract allows to the owner.

Consider removing the `TOKEN_SAVER_ROLE` in order to improve decentralization. Alternatively, ensure that `TOKEN_SAVER_ROLE` is handled by a multisig.
