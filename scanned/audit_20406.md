# [M-10] gracePeriod not increased after membership extension

## Summary
A bug was found in the FSDNetwork.sol function purchaseMembership. This bug causes the membership[msg.sender].gracePeriod to not be increased when the membership is extended. This could lead to a gracePeriod that is less than expected. The proof of concept was provided in the report, and the recommended mitigation step is to check if the gracePeriod should be increased and add the logic to do that if needed.

## Details
## Handle

## Vulnerability details

## Impact
In the function purchaseMembership of FSDNetwork.sol, when the membership is extended then membership[msg.sender].creation is increased, however 
membership[msg.sender].gracePeriod is not increased.
This might lead to a gracePeriod than is less then expected.
It seems logical to also increase the gracePeriod
