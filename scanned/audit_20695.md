# AbstractPoolsFactory contract owner can transfer staked tokens to any address by calling method withdrawLPRewards and passing argument lpTokenContract that refers staking token contract.

## Summary
This bug report recommends that the contract owner should not be allowed to transfer staked tokens to any address. This means that the person in charge of the contract should not be able to move tokens that have been put aside for a specific purpose to another location. This recommendation is being made to prevent any potential issues or misuse of the tokens by the contract owner.

## Details
Recommendation:
Forbid contract owner to transfer staked tokens to any address.
