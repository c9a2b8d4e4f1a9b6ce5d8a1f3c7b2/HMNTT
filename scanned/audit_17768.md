# UF-1 | Centralization Risk

## Summary
This bug report is about a potential issue with the `owner` address of a contract. The address is not a multi-sig and has permissions for a variety of functions, which could be dangerous. It is recommended to make the `owner` a multi-sig and/or introduce a timelock for improved community oversight. The resolution is that the contract ownership will be changed to the multi-sig at `0x87f385d152944689f92Ed523e9e5E9Bd58Ea62ef` as acknowledged by Ultimate Fantoms.

This bug report is about a security issue with the `owner` address of a contract. The address is not a multi-sig, meaning that only one person can access the contract's functions. This could be dangerous as the address has permissions for functions such as `renounceOwnership`, `transferOwnership`, `setRoyaltyAddress`, `setSpiritRouter`, `updatePaintRouter`, `setBaseURI`, `setMintSize`, and `sweepEthToAddress`. To improve security, it is recommended to make the `owner` a multi-sig and/or introduce a timelock for improved community oversight. The resolution is that the contract ownership will be changed to the multi-sig at `0x87f385d152944689f92Ed523e9e5E9Bd58Ea62ef` as acknowledged by Ultimate Fantoms.

## Details
**Description**

The `owner` address, `0x3e522051a9b1958aa1e828ac24afba4a551df37d`, is not a multi-sig and has potentially dangerous permissions for `renounceOwnership`, `transferOwnership`, `setRoyaltyAddress`, `setSpiritRouter`, `updatePaintRouter`, `setBaseURI`, `setMintSize`, `sweepEthToAddress`.

**Recommendation**

Make the `owner` a multi-sig and/or introduce a timelock for improved community oversight.

**Resolution**

Ultimate Fantoms: Acknowledged, contract ownership will be changed to the multisig at
`0x87f385d152944689f92Ed523e9e5E9Bd58Ea62ef`.
