# Upgradeable `creditManager` params

## Summary
This bug report is about a potential issue with the creditManager params in the Gearbox Protocol. If the params are upgraded, it could lead to the liquidation of creditAccounts and break the logic of dependent protocols. To prevent this from happening, it is recommended to restrict the changing of the params.

## Details
##### Description
Upgrade of `creditManager` params may lead to `creditAccount`s liquidation or break dependent protocols logic.
https://github.com/Gearbox-protocol/gearbox-contracts/blob/0ac33ba87212ce056ac6b6357ad74161d417158a/contracts/credit/CreditManager.sol#L705
##### Recommendation
We recommend restricting params changing.
