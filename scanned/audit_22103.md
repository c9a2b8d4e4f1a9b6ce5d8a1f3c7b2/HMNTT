# Use safe math

## Summary
This bug report is about the use of unchecked math operations in code. Unchecked math operations can lead to unexpected results and errors, so it is important to use checked operations to ensure accuracy. One way to do this is by using a safe math library such as the one provided by OpenZeppelin. Pre-condition checks can also be performed on any math operation to ensure accuracy. It is important to use checked math operations to ensure accuracy and avoid unexpected results and errors in code.

## Details
There are many unchecked math operations in the code. It’s always better to be safe and perform checked operations. Consider [using a safe math library](https://github.com/OpenZeppelin/zeppelin-solidity/blob/master/contracts/SafeMath.sol#L6), or performing pre-condition checks on any math operation.
