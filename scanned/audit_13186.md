# Centralization of Pausable and possible Denial of Service

## Summary
This bug report describes a medium severity issue in the contract called FEYTraderJoeProduct. It can be exploited by a malicious admin to prevent users from withdrawing their funds for a long time, which is similar to a Denial of Service attack. The recommendation is to add more decentralization to the contract by implementing a governance mechanism or a multisig. The client has stated that they plan to use a multisig initially and then transition to a governance model. The bug has been resolved.

## Details
**Severity**: Medium

**Status**: Resolved

**Description**

In contract FEYTraderJoeProduct, The global and local pausable functionality can be exploited by a malicious admin to deny users from withdrawing their funds for a very long time. This would also be equivalent to a Denial of Service attack if carried out.


**Recommendation**: 

It is advised to add more decentralization to the contract and roles, such as using a governance mechanism or a multisig.
 
**Comments**: The client assured that they would be using a multisig initially before moving on to a Governance model.
